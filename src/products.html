Multi Millet Flour

LOGI Nutritional Multi Millet Flour is a premium blend of Ragi, Bajra, Jowar, Foxtail, and Almonds—carefully selected for their exceptional health benefits and superior taste. At LOGI, we are committed to maintaining the highest quality standards, sourcing each ingredient directly from certified organic farms where crops are cultivated naturally without harmful chemicals or pesticides. This ensures that every batch retains its purity, natural flavor, and nutritional richness. The combination of these ancient grains and almonds makes this flour a true superfood—naturally rich in fiber, protein, calcium, iron, and essential micronutrients. With its low glycemic index, it helps regulate blood sugar levels, making it a smart choice for diabetes management. The high dietary fiber aids in controlling appetite, supporting weight management and preventing obesity. Packed with antioxidants, vitamins, and minerals, it strengthens immunity, while its protein and amino acids contribute to muscle strength and overall vitality. LOGI Multi Millet Flour is also incredibly versatile—perfect for making soft rotis, chapatis, and phulkas, traditional South Indian dishes like idly and dosa, or even popular snacks such as dhokla. Every serving offers a delightful balance of taste and health, making it an ideal daily staple for the whole family.

Ragi Millet Flour

LOGI’s Nutritional Ragi Flour is a wholesome blend of Ragi, Rice Bran, and Almonds, crafted with utmost care to ensure both health and taste for our customers. Ragi, known as a powerhouse of calcium, iron, and dietary fiber, supports strong bones, improves digestion, and helps manage blood sugar levels. Rice Bran adds natural antioxidants, Vitamin E, and heart-friendly nutrients that promote cholesterol management and overall wellness. Almonds enrich the flour with healthy fats, protein, and essential vitamins that enhance energy and brain health. This unique combination not only makes it diabetic-friendly and nutrient-dense but also versatile in Indian kitchens. LOGI’s Nutritional Ragi Flour can be used to prepare Chapati, Phulka, Roti, and Ragi Mudda, as well as traditional breakfast items like Idli and Dosa, and even popular Indian snacks such as Dhokla and more, making every meal both healthy and delicious.
